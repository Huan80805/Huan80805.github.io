About Me

As an incoming MSCS student at Georgia Tech, my research centers on Trustworthy AI at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I am joining Georgia Tech after two-year RAship at Academia Sinica, where I worked with Dr. Lun-Wei Ku on building culture-aware safety classifiers and generating adversarial attacks for LLM security assessment. My earlier research involved speech processing under Prof. Hung-yi Lee and model compression with Prof. Hao Tang.
