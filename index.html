About

Hi, I'm a research assistant at Academia Sinica, Taiwan, currently under the supervision of Dr. Lun-Wei Ku.
My research interest lies in Trustworthy AI, at the intersection of Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. During my time at Academia Sinica, I've culture-grounded safety classifiers and adversarial attack generations for LLM security assessment. I've also investigated fine-tuning attacks on LLMs.
Before joining Academia Sinica, I worked on speech processing and model compression under the supervision of Prof. Hung-yi Lee and Prof. Hao Tang respectively.
