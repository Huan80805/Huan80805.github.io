Taiwan visual-language model 70B (ongoing project)

Description

We continually pre-trained ViT-L + Llama3-Taiwan-70B (70B) grounded in Taiwanese culture.
Our pre-training data includes image-txt pairs, and image-text-interleaved entries. 50M images and 3B text tokens intotal.
We followed LLaVA-style architecture, where images are projected with vision encoder and concatenated with language embeddings. During development, we discovered that the model maintains instruction-following capabilities even without explicit instruction tuning.
