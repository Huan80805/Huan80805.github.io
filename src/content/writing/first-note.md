This site works better when each section answers a different question.

- `Home`, `Now`, and `Timeline` answer who I am and what I am focused on.
- `Projects` and `Writing` answer how I work and think.
- `Shelf` answers what I return to when I am not writing a paper or building a system.

That separation makes the site feel broader without becoming vague.
